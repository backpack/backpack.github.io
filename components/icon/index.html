Icon

A suite of icons for representing concepts, features and actions. Looking for the icon set? Check out the design tab for the list of available icons.

Skyscanner's icons are designed to be simple, modern, friendly, and sometimes quirky. Each icon is reduced to its minimal form, expressing essential characteristics.

Find the right icon

On web, icons are available at 18px and 24px. In apps, icons are available at 16px and 24px.

Some icons are only available at one size.
